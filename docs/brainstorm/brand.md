# Amana — Brand Brief

## Name

**Amana** — Hausa, Arabic-rooted: "trust, safekeeping." Pan-Nigerian recognition.

> Still needs IP / domain search and 10–15-user validation in week 1 of build. Swap if it doesn't land.

## Pillars

1. **Trust before transaction.**
2. **Calm, not flashy.**
3. **Specifically Nigerian.**
4. **Phone-to-phone is the mechanic, not the slogan** — show, don't say.
5. **Premium feel at accessible price.**

## Positioning lines

- **Primary (B):** *"For the people you trust with money — and the rules you shouldn't have to argue about."*
- **Paid-acquisition variant (A):** *"Stop guessing where your money went."*
- **App-store / demo trailer (C):** *"Two phones. One wallet. Total clarity."*
